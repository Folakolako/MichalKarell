  • Technology Integration

    In my teaching practice, I seamlessly integrate technology to enhance student learning and engagement. I regularly utilize a variety of educational technology tools, including Google Classroom, Microsoft Teams, or Edmodo to create interactive and collaborative learning experiences. By leveraging these tools, I can facilitate real-time feedback, foster a more connected classroom, and provide differentiated instruction tailored to individual student needs. I also incorporate subject-specific software and applications, such as CAD programs for design or coding platforms for tech education, which help students develop practical skills that are essential for their future careers. My commitment to staying updated with the latest educational technologies ensures that I can continually adapt my teaching strategies to meet the evolving needs of my students.
