  • Hi there! I’m in my third year at Australian Catholic University, pursuing a Bachelor of Secondary Education. My passion for teaching drives me to make a meaningful impact in the lives of future students, and I’m eager to continue learning and growing in this field. Outside of my academic pursuits, I’m all about adventure and exploration. I love to travel and experience new places, and I’m always up for a challenge. My hobbies include swimming, playing chess, video games, and rock climbing. I also have a knack for working with timber—there’s something incredibly satisfying about creating something with your own hands. When it comes to laser tag, I’m a bit of a legend—I almost always come out on top with the highest scores! Recently, I’ve also started learning the basics of the fujara, a traditional Slovakian flute, which has been an exciting new challenge for me.
